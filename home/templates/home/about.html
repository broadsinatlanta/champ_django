Usage

Champ is a web app used to auto generate complete, bespoke workouts for the user from a rich database of exercises.

3 Steps:
  1. Choose your goal:
    • Strength - Low reps, high weight. Choose a weight you struggle to finish 5 reps with. 3 - 5 mins rest between sets.
    • Muscle Growth - Medium reps, medium weight. Choose a weight you struggle to do 12 reps with. 90 - 120 seconds rest between sets.
    • Stamina - High reps, lower weight. Choose a weight you struggle to do 15 reps with. 60 seconds rest between sets.
  2. Choose the target muscle group.
  3. Select how much equipment you have available.
    • Fully Equipped - Includes exercises using specialised equipment e.g. rope climbs, TRX etc.
    • Basic Equipment - Only includes exercises using basic equipment e.g. Barbells, dumbbells
    • No Equipment - Only includes exercises that use no equipment i.e. calisthenic exercises.
